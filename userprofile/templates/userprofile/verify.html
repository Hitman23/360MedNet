commentAbout 360MedNet

The last two decades have seen an unprecedented growth and uptake of mobile and internet use in Africa. Health care professionals are constantly faced with the challenge of keeping abreast of the latest research and best practices in medicine. The global workforce, with dissimilar experiences and differing approaches can deliver synergies. Platforms that bring together people who think differently; Have different skills; have access to varying resources or use different approaches but are able to network minds, can foster innovative solutions.

360MedNet seeks to leverage the massive mobile and internet connectivity among medical students and qualified health care professionals to harness partnerships. We believe that exploration of such a global networking space will create vital solutions through sharing of diverse ideas and opinions. The instant and timely connection provides for unabated avenue for generation of solutions that impact individual patient outcomes and student performance in real time.
